Our founding

The founding of a fitness company typically involves a passionate individual or group identifying a need within the health and wellness industry and deciding to create a solution for it. This process often begins with extensive market research to understand the target audience, competition, and potential opportunities for innovation. Once a viable concept is developed, founders typically create a business plan outlining their vision, objectives, target market, marketing strategy, and financial projections..
